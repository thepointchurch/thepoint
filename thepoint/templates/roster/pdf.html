The ministry team is responsible for coordinating the Point’s Sunday Morning Assembly Roster and the Point’s South-East Queensland / Northern NSW Churches Assist Rosters. If you are not able to serve as rostered you are requested to make arrangements for your own replacement (e.g. arrange a swap with someone else on the roster). Please be sure to advise the ministry team of any changes made to the roster. Any other issues pertaining to these rosters should be addressed to the ministry team. If the matter is urgent (e.g. unavailable to serve at late notice and unable to find your own replacement), please be sure to advise one of the ministry team of the situation directly by telephone (emails and text messages may not be received or detected in time in cases of urgency).
